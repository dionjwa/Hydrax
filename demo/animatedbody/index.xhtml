This is a demo of Hydrax. Each skeleton is a physics 'ball' randomly bouncing around. When a skeleton hits another, and the other is in front, the 'attack' animation is played. The demo shows off:
  • Identical game output in Flash and Html5 canvas.
  • Bitmap spritesheets
  • Spritesheet bodies (with states and actions)
  • Box2D physics
